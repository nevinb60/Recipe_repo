Apple Pie


Ingredients

  • Apple
  • Brown Sugar
  • Ketchup
  • Vinegar
  • Pie Crust
  • Lit Cigarettes

Steps

  1. Take apples and cut them into quarters
  2. Take pie crust and wrap each apple in pie crust
  3. Take lit cigarettes and heat apple pie combo, until golden brown and flakey
